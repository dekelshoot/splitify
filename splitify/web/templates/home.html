Splitify

Separate your songs into vocals and instrumentals

Use cases

Splitify is a powerful tool for music producers, DJs, podcasters, and anyone who wants to work with audio.

Music production

Create custom music arrangements by removing or isolating individual instruments

Karaoke creation

Make your own karaoke tracks by removing the vocals from your favorite songs

DJ mixing

Mix songs together without the vocals, or use just the vocals over different music

Podcast editing

Remove background music from speech to make your podcast episodes more engaging

How does it work?

Step 1: Upload an MP3 file of the song you want to separate. Step 2: We'll use AI to analyze the music and create two new audio files: one with just the vocals and one with just the instrumental. Step 3: Download your new audio files and use them however you like!
